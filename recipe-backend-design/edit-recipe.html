Ingredient List:

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2-1/4 cups flour
  • 300 g semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Beat butter, sugars, eggs, and vanilla in large bowl until light and fluffy. Stir in flour and baking soda until well blended. Add chocolate chips; mix well.
  3. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough, 2 inches a part, onto baking sheets.
  4. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
  5. Cool on baking sheet for 3 minutes. Remove to wire racks; cool completely.
